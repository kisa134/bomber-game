# BomberMeme.fun — Frequently Asked Questions

*Blow up your friends · a real-time PvP bomber arena on Solana.*

## Getting Started

### What is BomberMeme.fun?
BomberMeme.fun is a fast, real-time multiplayer bomber-arena game. Up to **4
players** drop bombs on a grid, blow up blocks and each other, grab power-ups,
and the **last one standing wins**. You can play for free, practice against bots,
or compete for real **$BOMB** tokens.

### How do I start playing?
1. Open the game in your browser (or inside Telegram).
2. Pick a nickname.
3. Hit **Practice vs bots** to learn the ropes, or **Play Online** to jump into
   live matches.
4. To play for real tokens, connect a Solana wallet and deposit $BOMB.

### Do I need a wallet to play?
No. You can play **Practice** (vs bots) and **Casual** (free chips) without a
wallet. You only need to connect a Solana wallet to play staked matches for real
**$BOMB** tokens or to withdraw winnings.

### Is it free to play?
Yes. Practice and casual chip matches are completely free. Real-token (Arena)
matches require a stake, which you choose.

## Gameplay

### How do I win a match?
Be the **last player alive**. Trap and blow up your opponents with bombs, or
push them into danger. If time runs out, the match enters **sudden death**.

### What are the controls?
- **Desktop:** Arrow keys or **WASD** to move, **Space** to drop a bomb.
- **Mobile:** an on-screen joystick or D-pad to move, and a bomb button. You can
  switch between joystick and buttons in Settings.

### What are power-ups?
Blowing up soft blocks can drop power-ups that make you stronger for the match —
more bombs at once, bigger blast range, faster movement, and more. Grab them to
gain an edge.

### What is "first blood"?
The first player-on-player hit of the match earns a bonus — a big callout and an
instant random power-up. Be aggressive early.

### What happens on a draw?
If no single player survives (e.g. everyone dies at once or time fully expires
with multiple players), it's a **draw**. In staked matches, **all stakes are
refunded** in full — the house takes nothing on a draw.

### Can I play on my phone?
Yes. The game is mobile-first and also runs inside Telegram. Landscape is
recommended — the board is wide, so the game will prompt you to rotate.

## Game Modes

### What modes are there?
- **Practice vs bots** — solo training. Choose the bot difficulty (Easy / Normal
  / Hard) and how many bots (1–3). Free, no stakes.
- **Casual** — quick matches for free **chips**.
- **The Arena** — staked matches for real **$BOMB** tokens. Winner takes the pot.
- **Create a lobby** — open your own room (chips or token), pick the stake, and
  invite friends with a room code.

### How do I play with friends?
Create a lobby and share the **room code**, or send your invite link. Your
friends enter the code in the lobby browser to join your room.

### Can I watch live matches?
Yes. Matches in progress show up in the lobby browser as **LIVE** — tap to
spectate.

### What's "Quick Match"?
One tap to get matched into an open room at your chosen stake — the fastest way
into a game.

## Currencies

### What's the difference between Chips and $BOMB?
- **Chips 🪙** are a free, in-game currency for casual play. They can't be
  withdrawn and have no cash value — just for fun and bragging rights.
- **$BOMB 💎** is the real Solana token. You deposit it, play staked matches for
  it, and can withdraw your balance to your wallet.

### How do I get chips?
Chips are granted to your account and earned through play. They're free.

### Where does my $BOMB balance live?
When you deposit, your tokens are held in the game's **treasury** and credited to
your in-game balance (a custodial ledger). You can withdraw back to your wallet
at any time, subject to the displayed limits.

## Staking, Payouts & Fees

### How do staked (Arena) matches work?
Every player puts up the same **stake**. All stakes form the **pot**. The
**winner takes the pot, minus a small house rake**. On a draw, everyone is
refunded.

### What is the house rake?
A small commission taken from the pot of staked matches (the house's only fee).
For example, at a 5% rake, the winner of a 100-token pot receives 95 tokens. The
exact rake is shown in-game.

### Where does the rake go?
The rake funds the ecosystem: a share is paid out to the **referral network**
(see below), and the rest supports the platform (servers, development, rewards,
and token mechanics). See the Tokenomics page for the full breakdown.

### Can I lose tokens I didn't stake?
No. You only ever risk the stake you choose for a match. Nothing is taken from
your balance beyond the stake you agree to.

### What if a match is interrupted (server restart, crash)?
Your stake is protected. If a staked match is interrupted before it settles, the
escrowed stakes are **refunded**. Funds are never stranded.

## Wallet, Deposits & Withdrawals

### Which wallets are supported?
Any standard Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, OKX, Coinbase Wallet,
and others) via the Solana Wallet Standard. On mobile, the game uses wallet
deeplinks; inside Telegram it connects through Phantom.

### How do I deposit $BOMB?
Connect your wallet, open the Bank, enter an amount, and approve the transaction.
Your tokens go to the treasury and your in-game balance is credited once the
transfer confirms.

### My deposit hasn't shown up — what do I do?
Deposits are usually credited automatically within seconds. If it's delayed, you
can **claim it manually** by its transaction signature in the Bank. The system
credits the wallet that sent the transfer and prevents the same deposit from
being credited twice.

### How do I withdraw?
Open the Bank and request a withdrawal. Tokens are sent from the treasury to your
connected wallet. There are minimum and maximum withdrawal limits, shown in-app.

### Is my withdrawal safe if something goes wrong?
Yes. Withdrawals are guarded so a transaction is never paid twice, and your
balance is never over-debited. If a withdrawal is sent but not yet confirmed, the
game tells you to check your wallet rather than retrying automatically.

## Referrals (Invite & Earn)

### How does the referral program work?
Share your invite link. Anyone who joins through it is permanently linked to you.
When your network plays **staked token matches**, you earn a share of the house
rake — **5 levels deep**.

### How much do I earn?
Referral rewards are paid from the rake across 5 levels:
**Level 1: 10% · Level 2: 5% · Level 3: 3% · Level 4: 2% · Level 5: 1%** (of the
rake). That means you earn not just from people you invite directly, but from
everyone they invite, down 5 levels.

### When do I get paid?
Rewards are credited in real **$BOMB** to your balance automatically when your
network plays a staked token match that ends with a winner. Earnings are
withdrawable like any other balance.

### Do casual/chip or practice games earn referral rewards?
No. Referral rewards come from the rake on **real-token** matches only. Free
chips and practice games don't generate rake.

### Can I refer myself or change who referred me?
No. You can't refer your own wallet, and once you're linked to a referrer it's
permanent.

## Rating & Leaderboards

### How does rating work?
Every ranked match updates your **global rating** using an Elo system — beat
stronger players to climb faster. As your rating grows you move through leagues:
**Beginner → Advanced → Pro → Champion**.

### What leaderboards are there?
Three boards: **Rating** (skill), **Tokens won** (lifetime real-token winnings),
and **Chips won** (lifetime free-currency winnings).

### Do bots or practice affect my rating?
No. Practice matches against bots don't change your rating or leaderboard
standing.

## Fairness & Security

### Are matches fair / not rigged?
Yes. The game map is **provably fair**: the server commits to a hashed random
seed at the start of each match and **reveals** the seed at the end, so anyone
can verify the map wasn't manipulated.

### Is the game server-authoritative?
Yes. All gameplay, stats, and balances are computed and stored on the server, so
they can't be faked or inflated by a modified client.

### Are my funds safe?
Funds are held in a custodial treasury with an atomic ledger: balances can't go
negative, deposits can't be double-credited, withdrawals can't be double-paid,
and interrupted staked matches are refunded.

## Troubleshooting

### The game looks outdated after an update.
Do a hard refresh (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + R). The game is a PWA and may have cached
an older version.

### I got disconnected mid-match — did I lose?
If you reconnect within the short grace window, you're dropped right back into
your match. Staked funds are protected either way.

### "Server full" — what does that mean?
At peak load the server temporarily stops opening new rooms to keep existing
matches running smoothly. Try again shortly.

### My nickname is taken.
Nicknames are globally unique — pick another.

### I connected a wallet but can't join a token match.
Make sure you've deposited enough $BOMB to cover the stake. Staked token matches
require a funded balance.
